About Us


AL-REHMAN TRADING COMPANY IMPORTERS - EXPORTERS | CUSTOM CLEARING AND FORWARDING AGENTS


Welcome to AL-REHMAN TRADING COMPANY, your trusted partner in the dynamic and ever-evolving world of international trade. With a strategic presence across key global regions, including Pakistan, China, the USA, India, Afghanistan, and Central Asian states, we bring decades of experience and expertise to support multinational and national companies, importers, and exporters.

Located at the vital crossroads in Peshawar, Pakistan, our comprehensive network of offices spans Sea Ports, Dry Ports, Air Ports, and international borders. Our professional team is dedicated to providing top-notch services in shipping, logistics, and customs clearing & forwarding, catering to shipments of all sizes, nature, and quantities.

As a forward-thinking company, AL-REHMAN TRADING COMPANY continuously adapts to the evolving political and economic landscape in Pakistan. We have expanded our operations to include Import, Export, Customs Clearance, Forwarding, and the handling of Transit Shipments to Afghanistan. Leveraging Pakistan’s strategic geographic position, we offer efficient and reliable transit routes for landlocked Afghanistan, ensuring the shortest possible distance for cargo transit.

Our services are designed to meet the needs of a diverse clientele, including Government contractors, Private Sector contractors, Public/Private organizations, and NGOs. We specialize in arranging comprehensive freight solutions, including transport to major Afghan cities such as Kabul, Jalalabad, and Bagram. Our capabilities extend from inland transport to complete international freight solutions through our extensive global network.

At AL-REHMAN TRADING COMPANY, we handle a wide range of shipments, from household goods and personal effects to foodstuffs, vehicles, machinery, and equipment essential for Afghanistan’s reconstruction and relief efforts. We meticulously follow all necessary formalities to ensure smooth processing and minimize additional costs.

We welcome inquiries from governmental organizations, companies, NGOs, individuals, and freight forwarders. Our commitment to reliability, quick response, and regular status updates ensures that your goods are in safe hands.


We look forward to the opportunity to serve you with excellence and dedication.
